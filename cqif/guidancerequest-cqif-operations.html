guidance

The guidance operation requests clinical decision support guidance based on a specific knowledge module

Formal Definition (as a OperationDefinition).

URL: [base]/$guidance

In Parameters:

Out Parameters:
NameCardinalityTypeBindingProfileDocumentation

Out Parameters:
